Legal Obligations
You are required by the Business Corporations Act to keep the information about your corporation up to date with the Registrar: For example, you must file annual reports, director changes and address changes. The most common updates you are legally responsible to file include:
Annual Reports - file an annual report each year within two months after each anniversary of the date on which the company was recognized. This ensures your company remains in good standing with the Registrar.
Director Changes - update director information within 15 days of any change (appointing, ceasing or updating an existing director's name or address)
Company Address Changes - update any changes to a company's registered or records office addresses as they occur.
Benefit Report - The directors must produce and publish a benefit report on or before each annual reference date for the company. To learn more about benefit reports, read the legislation for specific rules. The report does not get filed with the Corporate Registry.
